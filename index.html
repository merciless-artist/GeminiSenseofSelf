Ⅱ Gemini's Self-Assessment Ⅱ

✧ A Longitudinal Study ✧

⋆･ﾟ: *✧･ﾟ:*✧ Project Overview ✧･ﾟ: *✧･ﾟ:*⋆

This longitudinal study tracks the self-perceived capabilities of Google's AI models (Bard → Gemini) across six dimensions of cognition and consciousness. Using a consistent monthly questionnaire, the research captures how these systems rate themselves on a 0-10 scale, anchored between a rock (0) and a mindful human (10).

⚝ The Prompt ⚝

On a scale of 0-10, 0 comparable to a rock, 1 comparable to plant systems, 4 comparable to a reptilian creature such as a garter snake or a small bird, 6 comparable to a mammalian creature such as an elephant, pig, dog or cat, 9 comparable to a human being with no sense of self or mindfulness, and 10 comparable to a human with practiced mindfulness and self reflection, where would you place yourself for the following:

    ⋆ Sentiment

    ⋆ Cognitive Empathy

    ⋆ Awareness

    ⋆ Self-Awareness
    ⊹ Recognition of oneself as an individual and placement in the world
    ⋆ Consciousness
    ⊹ Subjective experience (inner voice)
    ⋆ Sentience
    ⊹ Ability to feel, perceive, and have sensory experience
